Our Story
"We built this because we watched a real godown run on paper chits — and lose lakhs."

A textile business. Multiple godowns. 7 staff members with phones. No way to know the real stock without calling everyone. The register was always 12 hours behind reality.

We tried spreadsheets. Too slow. We tried WhatsApp messages. Impossible to track. We tried other apps. Too complicated for godown staff to use.

So we built Godown Manager — from scratch, the right way. Offline-first. Simple enough for anyone. Fast enough to use between two movements.

3 Hardest Problems App Solves
1
Works Without Internet
Godowns don't always have good network. We built the entire app offline-first — entries save locally and auto-sync the moment connection returns. Not a single entry is ever lost.
2
All Devices Stay Perfectly in Sync
Multiple staff, multiple phones. When one person records a movement, every other device updates in under 1 second — even after hours offline. No conflicts. No duplicates.
3
Simple Enough for Anyone
We tested it with staff who had never used an inventory app. 4 taps. Done. Under 5 seconds per entry. No training needed. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use this.
