What Is Godown Manager?

In Short: Godown Manager is a real-time mobile inventory management tool built for small wholesale and trading businesses in India.

Godown Manager is a mobile inventory management application developed and operated by FutureTech. The App is designed to help small wholesale businesses — including but not limited to textile traders, grocery distributors, hardware merchants, and general trading businesses — manage stock across multiple godowns and shop locations.

Core features of the Services include:

  • Real-time stock tracking — every goods movement (inward, outward, transfer, or delivery) is recorded the moment it occurs and synced across all connected devices simultaneously.
  • Multi-location management — stock levels across multiple godowns and retail shops are tracked in a single unified interface.
  • Staff access — multiple staff members can be added to a business account, each recording their respective transactions.
  • Transaction history & logs — a complete, time-stamped audit trail of all stock movements.
  • Low-stock alerts — automated notifications when inventory falls below defined thresholds.

The Services are intended to replace paper-based register systems currently used by small wholesale businesses. The App is not an accounting software, invoicing platform, GST filing tool, or financial advisory service. Any figures displayed in the App are for inventory tracking purposes only and should not be relied upon for tax, financial, or compliance purposes without independent verification.
